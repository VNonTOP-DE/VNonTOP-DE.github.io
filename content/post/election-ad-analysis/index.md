## Project Overview

This project analyzes the advertising strategies employed by Donald Trump and Kamala Harris during the 2024 U.S. presidential campaign using data from the Facebook Ad Library. ## Results & Impact

### Key Findings
1. **Spending Efficiency:**
   - Harris showed higher cost efficiency in Michigan and Nevada
   - Trump's targeted approach proved more effective in battleground states

2. **Demographic Insights:**
   - Harris: Stronger engagement with younger voters
   - Trump: Better performance with older demographics

3. **ROI Analysis:**
   - Identified optimal ad spend levels by region
   - Developed predictive models for engagement rates
